WeRateDogs act_report¶

Introduction¶

WeRateDogs is a twitter account that rates people's dogs with humorous comment about dogs, has 4million followers and has received international recognition. With the help of Udacity, I gathered WeRateDogs twitter archive data. It was in 3 different datasets, the enhanced twitter archive, image description file and the tweet json txt file.

This real world data is hardly clean and therefore a wrangling process is performed to ensure data quality and tidiness which I did for the WeRateDogs project. I then analyzed and visualized the data as shown below:

Distribution of Rating¶

The diagram shows the rating distribution which is skewed to the left with high rating ranging between 9 and 13.

Most used Twitter Source¶

The diagram clearly shows that the most used twitter source is the Iphone, then Vine, third being twitter for web client and the least used source is the tweetdeck.

Most Rated Dog stage¶

  • The most rated dog stage is the doggo puppo, followed by puppo, floofer and the doggo. The diagram show the doggo puppo with the highest rating of over 12.

10 Most Popular Dog breeds¶

Golden retriever is the most popular dog breed. Other popular dog breeds are labrador retriever, pembroke, chihuahua while the least famous breeds are pomeranian and cocker spanier.

Correlation between Retweet count and Favorite¶

  • The scatterplot above shows, there is a positive correlation between the Favorite count and the retweet count which means when one of the favorite increases the retweet count increase too. There is a positive connection between the two variables.
